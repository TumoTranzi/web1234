Lesotho Sun

The Lesotho Sun hotel is a prominent hotel located in Maseru, the capital city of Lesotho. It is known for its luxurious accommodations and amenities, catering primarily to business and leisure travelers. The hotel offers a range of facilities including restaurants, bars, conference rooms, and leisure facilities such as a swimming pool and spa services. Its central location makes it convenient for exploring the city and experiencing the cultural and natural attractions of Lesotho.

Maliba Lodge

Maliba Lodge is a luxury eco-lodge located in the picturesque Maluti Mountains of Lesotho, near the Tsehlanyane National Park. Offering stunning views and a serene environment, it features well-appointed lodges and chalets, along with a restaurant, bar, and wellness facilities. Guests can engage in various outdoor activities, including hiking and horseback riding, while enjoying the region's natural beauty. Maliba Lodge emphasizes sustainable tourism and provides a unique blend of comfort and adventure for travelers seeking an immersive experience in nature.

Mountain View

Mountain View Hotel is situated in the picturesque town of Maseru, Lesotho, offering stunning views of the surrounding mountains. The hotel features comfortable accommodations, a restaurant serving local and international cuisine, and facilities for conferences and events. With a focus on hospitality and convenience, it provides an inviting atmosphere for both business and leisure travelers looking to explore the beauty of Lesotho.

Avani Hotel

Avani Lesotho Hotel & Casino is a modern hotel located in Maseru, offering stylish accommodations and a range of amenities. Guests can enjoy a casino, a restaurant, a bar, and a wellness center, along with meeting and event spaces. The hotel's contemporary design, combined with stunning views of the Maluti Mountains, creates a welcoming atmosphere for both business and leisure travelers. Its convenient location allows easy access to local attractions and cultural experiences.
